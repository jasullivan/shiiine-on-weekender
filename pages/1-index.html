2017 Line Up

Happy Mondays //

The Happy Mondays, legends of the Madchester scene were originally discovered by Tony Wilson at his infamous Hacienda nightclub. Their first album debuted in 1987 followed by their second in 1988 and their seminal "Pills 'n' Thrills and Bellyaches" in 1990.

Levellers //

The Levellers were formed in 1988 by Mark Chadwick and Jeremy Cunningham. Adding Charlie Heather, Jon Sevink and Alan Miles. Their first album A Weapon Called the Word went gold while their following album Levelling the Land went platinum and was cited a classic of the early 1990s.

The Wonder Stuff //

The Wonder Stuff formed in 1986 with the original line-up of Miles Hunt, Malcom Treece, Rob Jones and Martin Gilks. 1989 saw the release of "Hup" and "Never Loved Elvis" in 1991 which proved their biggest success.

The Farm //

Legendary Liverppol band The Farm formed in 1983 but it wasn't until the release of their 1990 single "Groovy Train" that they found success. After their biggest hit "All Together Now" they released their debut album "Spartacus" which stormed to the top of the UK album charts in 1991.
